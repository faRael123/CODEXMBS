About Gajoda

General information about the transport service, the fleet, and the people behind daily operations.

Company Overview

Gajoda Transportation Services operates as a local public transport provider serving commuters who travel between terminal points and nearby communities through minibus-based daily operations.

Main Service Area

The current platform is focused on the Cabiao to Cabanatuan service corridor and nearby commuting activity, with placeholders ready for final terminal, office, and route coverage details.

Drivers

Conductors

Dispatch and Management
